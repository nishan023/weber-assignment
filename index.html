Welcome to My Autobiography Webpage!

Hello! My name is Sandeep Dhakal. This webpage is all about me and my interests. I'm excited to share my story with you. I am originally from Nepal. I passed my primary education from Champabot Higher Secondary School. After that, I completed my high school from St. Lawrence College, located in Kathmandu. Currently, I am enrolled as a student at Weber State University. Since childhood, I have been interested in singing and playing table tennis. In the future, I aspire to become a web developer.

Getting to Know Sandeep Dhakal

If you could have one superpower, what would it be and why?

If I could have one superpower, it would be the ability to fly. Flying would give me the freedom to explore the world from a different perspective and travel to places quickly and effortlessly. It would also provide a sense of exhilaration and adventure that I imagine would be unmatched by any other experience.
